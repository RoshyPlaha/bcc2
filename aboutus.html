About Us

Hello - I'm Rosh. I've been in Blackheath for almost 4 years now. Having owned a fair few cars, I've always enjoyed taking them to meet-ups when possible. However, although [we] all love driving, its normally a bit of schlep to get to the other car meets around London and beyond.

The mission is to establish a car community in the heart of Blackheath. My neck has sharply swivelled more than a few times by some of the great cars Ive seen in the area. We definitely have an untapped opportunity here.

So, with a coffee in hand, and probably some cake - feel free to sign up to the waitlist as we look to host a meet-up this fall near the village. Lastly, if you see me around the heath - feel free to say hi!
